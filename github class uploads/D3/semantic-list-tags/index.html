Slow Cooker Beef Stew

Content borrowed from: thecozycook.com

Ingredients

  • 2 lbs beef stew meat, cut into 1" cubes
  • 1/4 cup al-purpose flour
  • 1/2 tsp salt
  • 1/2 tsp ground black pepper
  • 1 clove garlic; minced
  • 1 bay leaf
  • 1 tsp paprika
  • 1 tsp Worcestershire sauce
  • 1 onion, chopped
  • 1 1/2 cups beef broth
  • 2 potatoes, diced
  • 4 carrots, sliced
  • 1 stalk celery, chopped

Directions

  1. Place meat in slow cooker; add garlic, bay leaf, paprika, Worcestershire sauce, onion, broth, potatoes, carrots, and celery.
  2. In a small bowl, mix flour, salt, and pepper; sprinkle over meat and stir to coat.
  3. Cover and cook on Low for 10–12 hours or on High for 4–6 hours.
  4. Remove bay leaf, stir gently, and serve hot.
